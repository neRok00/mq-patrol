Stock Steering Info

    Tagged with:
  • Steering

Table of Contents

The steering system is the same across all MQ Patrols. They were available in either manual or power steer. They feature a multi link/arm system (ie drag link) from the steering box (pitman arm) to the wheel (tie-rod). A steering damper is connected off one of the links. The wheels are joined via a conventional tie-rod.
