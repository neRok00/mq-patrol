The following information relates to the starter motors used on all 160/MQ/MK Patrols, and also GQ/Y60 Patrols. The table can be used for cross-referencing if a starter from another model Patrol can be used in your own.

Info supplied by jwaye2 on Patrol4x4 forum, see: http://www.patrol4x4.com/forum/nissan-patrol-mq-mk-12/will-gq-starter-motor-fit-into-mk-250978

As you will determine from the table below, the GQ starter motors have different specs to all the MQ motors, and hence are not usable.

Engine Flange Diameter (mm) Pintion Diameter (mm) Pinion at rest (mm) Pinion Teeth Mount center A (mm)
L28 v81 82 28 8 8 110
L28 v92 82 29 10 9 110
P40 82 29 31 9 105
SD33 + SD33-T 105 40 12 11 1393
TB42 82 35 12 9 110
TD42 105 40 10 1 1 1404
RB30 v85 82 28 8 8 110
RB30 v96 82 29 10 9 110
  1. L28 has 2 different versions, possibly for different gearbox/flywheel combinations (eg MQ 4 speed vs MK 5 speed?). [return]
  2. L28 has 2 different versions, possibly for different gearbox/flywheel combinations (eg MQ 4 speed vs MK 5 speed?). [return]
  3. SD33 Mount center B = 138mm, Mount center C = 93mm. [return]
  4. TD42 Mount center B = 115mm, Mount center C = 80mm. [return]
  5. RB30 has 2 different versions, possibly for different transmissions (auto vs manual?).
    NOTE: Whilst they have the same measurements as MQ L28, the nose-cone is in a different position, and hence it does not interchange. [return]
  6. RB30 has 2 different versions, possibly for different transmissions (auto vs manual?).
    NOTE: Whilst they have the same measurements as MQ L28, the nose-cone is in a different position, and hence it does not interchange. [return]
